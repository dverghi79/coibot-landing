The problem

That sub's insurance expired 3 weeks ago. You found out from the adjuster.

Manual tracking is a liability trap.

You have 47 subs on your books. 12 of their COIs expire this month. Your office manager is tracking them in a spreadsheet she updates when she remembers.

ACORD 25 assembly = time sink.

Pull up the policy in AMS360, cross-reference limits, type them into the ACORD form, double-check every field. One certificate takes 10–15 minutes. Multiply by dozens per week.

AMS360's native tool doesn't cut it.

Vertafore's certificate tool has accuracy problems documented on G2. You end up checking its output anyway — doing the work twice.

Step 1

See every expired COI instantly

COIBot links to your AMS360 instance and reads policy data directly. Within minutes, you'll see which COIs are current, which are expiring, and which already lapsed.

Step 2

Generate perfect ACORD 25s

Pick the insured, choose the holder, and every ACORD 25 field is pre-filled with correct limits and additional insureds. Review in 12 seconds, click generate.

Step 3

Deliver instantly, zero correction requests

COIBot emails the PDF to the holder. No re-typing, no errors, no callbacks. Your producers get perfect certs the first time, every time.
